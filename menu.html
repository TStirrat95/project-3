Drinks & Food

Maritime IPA

Meaning, bordering the sea, this Maritime IPA has notes of both land and sea. The Florida grown hops are complimented by tropical papaya with a dash of sea salt to make a smooth sailing IPA.

PSJ Pale Ale

The PSJ Pale Ale is fondly named after Port St. Joe, Florida. Like PSJ, this Pale Ale is easy going. Notes of key lime and mango make for a beer that you can drink on any beach.

Salty Sailor Pretzel Bites

Missing the salty air of the ocean? The Salty Sailor Pretzel Bites are crusted with sea salt and served with a pimento cheese infused with our PSJ Pale Ale.
