Our Story

Anchored Brewing Co. started in a garage with a home-brew kit and a dream. Founder and CEO, Tyler Stirrat had an idea to combine his two loves of beer and the ocean into one, unique brewery. This brewery would be like no other brewery you had seen before. Anchored Brewing Co. would have a calming, relaxed vibe where you could enjoy a beer and a conversation with a friend.

Fast forward two years and Anchored Brewing Co. now has three locations in North Florida and South Georgia and provides its nautically inspired beer across the nation. While we may have grown, we still put the same love and attention into our beer as Tyler did when he was making every batch by hand in his garage.
